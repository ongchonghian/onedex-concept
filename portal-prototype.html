Glossary

Canonical vocabulary for the unified portal. Auto-generated from CONTEXT.md at build time. Old → new mappings shown for migration users.

Agreement

A user-visible consent record. Replaces: Subscription, DER, SPR, Client Relation.

Direct Agreement

An Agreement between two counterparties, no intermediary. Replaces: DER.

Service-Provider Agreement

A party appoints a third party to send or receive on their behalf. Replaces: SPR.

Inbox

The home view — items needing action. Split into Mine and My team's. Replaces: Dashboard.

DEX

A regulated data-exchange domain. URL-anchored at /portal/<dex>. Aggregated view at /portal/all.

Agreement Template

Org-scoped, DEX-scoped, mutable blueprint surfaced after ≥3 similar Agreements.
