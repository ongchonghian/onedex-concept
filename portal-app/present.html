07 · East Arm · What launches first vs what's deferred

Honest scoping.

v1 · Six months

  • One portal, sign-in unified
  • Inbox home + team-claim
  • One Messages page
  • Live timeline + retry/close
  • One Composer + auto-review
  • Watch toggle + digest
  • Cross-network warnings
  • Settings → Pitstops page

Deferred · Needs platform

  • Schema-negotiation handshake
  • In-place Agreement amendments
  • Bulk send (one→many)
  • Test mode
  • Org-wide failure banner
  • Mobile push

Deferred items all wait on one shared coordination layer.

10 · The Town Hall · What we need from leadership

Three calls before the build begins.

A

Confirm who can send data on each Agreement

Only data owners (or SPs explicitly acting on their behalf) compose Messages. Today's system is more permissive.

Product Lead + Compliance · before kickoff

B

Confirm "revoke and recreate" for schema changes

In-place amendments arrive later, when the coordination layer ships. Acceptable for the first customer launch?

Product Lead + Engineering Lead · before kickoff

C

Greenlight a one-week user-test round

5–6 operators · 3 sample tasks each. A week of design + an afternoon of sessions. Validates the consolidated Messages page + Composer before build.

UIUX Lead + Product Lead · within 2 weeks

Decisions today. Build kicks off in 2 weeks. v1 ships in 6 months.
